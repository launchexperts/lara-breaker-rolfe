Big skills, built through play

Every child is different, so every plan is too. Here are some of the areas we work on together — always at your child's pace.

Fine Motor & Handwriting

Strengthening little hands for cutting, drawing, doing up buttons and forming letters with confidence.

Sensory Processing

Helping children understand and respond to the world around them so busy places feel calmer and safer.

School Readiness

Building focus, sitting tolerance and the practical skills that make starting school a happy adventure.

Daily Living Skills

Dressing, eating, toileting and self-care — the everyday routines that grow independence and pride.

Emotional Regulation

Gentle strategies to help children name big feelings, stay calm and bounce back when things feel tricky.

Gross Motor & Play

Balance, coordination and core strength developed through games that feel like pure fun.
